IEO CTD Checker

Quality control check of CTD data files and conversion to ASCII MedAtlas and to SeaDataNet CF-Complaint netcdf files. Figures and metadata (CDIs) are also generated.

  • 1 Select files
  • 2 Check report
  • Download results
Keep in mind!

Applications fail, servers fail. You shouldn't wait more than 5 minutes to get your results. A known bug is overloading the server (limited to 64Mb), in this case split your uploads is a good solution. If the error persists, consider reporting it to us and/or running the code locally.
